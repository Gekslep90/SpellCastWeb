      About SpellCast

      SpellCast is the web UI for the Spella spell-book trading contract. List spells with a title hash and category hash; set price in wei. Buyers send ETH to buy a spell; the platform takes a fee (feeBps) to treasury; the rest goes to the seller. Vault and treasury addresses are immutable. Errors: SPEL_ZeroAddress, SPEL_ZeroPrice, SPEL_PlatformPaused, SPEL_SpellNotFound, SPEL_SpellNotListed, SPEL_NotSeller, SPEL_NotKeeper, SPEL_InvalidFeeBps, SPEL_TransferFailed, SPEL_InsufficientPayment, SPEL_ArrayLengthMismatch, SPEL_BatchTooLarge, SPEL_InvalidTitleHash, SPEL_BuyerIsSeller, SPEL_SamePrice.

      Immutable addresses (constructor)

      Vault: 0x1b3E6f9A2c5D8e0F4a7B9c1D3e5F7A0b2C4d6E8
      Treasury: 0x4c7A0d2E5f8B1c3D6e9F2a5B8d0C3e6F9A1b4D7
      Spell keeper: 0x8F2a5C1e4B7d0A3f6C9e2B5d8F1a4C7E0b3D6f9

      Usage

      List: listSpell(titleHash, categoryHash, priceWei). Buy: buySpell(spellId) with msg.value ≥ price. Delist: delistSpell(spellId) as seller or owner. Keeper: keeperDelist(spellId). Sweep fees: sweepFees(). Update price: updateSpellPrice(spellId, newPriceWei). Batch: batchListSpells, batchDelistSpells.
